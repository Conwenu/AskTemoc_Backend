Naveen Jindal School of Management
Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence and Supply Chain Management and Analytics (Double Major) (BS)
Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence and Supply Chain Management and Analytics (Double Major)

Degree Requirements (124 semester credit hours)

The Bachelor of Science in Business Analytics and Artificial Intelligence and Bachelor of Science in Supply Chain Management and Analytics double major is a minimum 124 semester credit hours degree program that prepares students to address business analytics issues in global supply chain markets. The program emphasizes building key skills and competencies of analytics professionals. Students also gain analytical decision-making and problem-solving skills through real-life consulting while working on global supply chain projects and internships.
