NOTES:
1	Curriculum Requirements can be fulfilled by other approved courses from accredited institutions of higher education. The courses listed are recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy both Core Curriculum and Major Requirements at UT Dallas.
2	Students completing the 020 requirement with a MATH course must complete ALEKS exam prior to enrollment. PHIL 2303 does not require the ALEKS exam.
3	VPA Requirement - 12 semester credit hours - VPAS 3340, VPAS 4389, (VPAS 4390 or VPAS 4V99) and 3 semester credit hours of upper-level Related Courses from the School of Arts, Humanities, and Technology (excluding the MUSI prefix).
4	16 semester credit hours of Applied Music courses in primary instrument or voice (at least 8 semester credit hours must be upper-division)
5	8 semester credit hours of any 1 semester credit hour upper-division MUSI ensemble participation. Placement by audition
6	Upper-Division MUSI History/Theory Elective - Select 6 semester credit hours of upper-division Music History/Theory elective courses (MUSI 3322, MUSI 3324, MUSI 3325, MUSI 3327, MUSI 3342, MUSI 3389, or MUSI 4348)
7	Free Elective - any lower or upper-level course. Students may need to take some as upper-level to meet the university requirement of earning a minimum of 45 semester credit hours of upper-level courses.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level or higher courses.
