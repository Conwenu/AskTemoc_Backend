NOTES:
1	Curriculum Requirements can be fulfilled by other approved courses from accredited institutions of higher education. The courses listed are recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy both Core Curriculum and Major Requirements at UT Dallas.
2	Students completing the 020 requirement with a MATH course must complete ALEKS exam prior to enrollment. PHIL 2303 does not require the ALEKS exam.
3	If the student takes THEA 1310 as their 050 core, then the student may choose any free elective in its place as a lower-level major requirement.
4	Lower-Division Major Req - 7 semester credit hours beyond Core Curriculum - AHTC 1100 and THEA 1310, and 3 semester credit hours from a THEA studio course, including: (THEA 1351, THEA 1352, THEA 2311, THEA 2350, THEA 2364, THEA 2371, THEA 2372, THEA 2373, or THEA 2V71), and 3 semester credit hours from a THEA/DANC/MUSI studio ensemble course. Students may substitute 3 of the 6 required semester credit hours with MUSI or DANC studio courses. NOTE: If the student took one of these courses as their core, then the student may choose any free elective in its place as a lower-level major requirement.
5	Major Core - 12 semester credit hours - VPAS 3340 and VPAS 4389 and (VPAS 4390 or VPAS 4V99) and 3 semester credit hours of upper-level Related Courses from AHTC (excluding the THEA prefix).
6	Concentration - 24 semester credit hours - THEA 3323 and (THEA 3310 or 3 semester credit hours of THEA 3V10) and 12 semester credit hours of upper-level THEA courses and 3 semester credit hours of upper-division THEA/DANC/MUSI courses and 3 semester credit hours of upper-division Visual and Performing Arts elective.
7	Free Elective - 35 semester credit hours - any lower or upper-level course. Students may need to take some as upper-level to meet the university requirement of earning a minimum of 45 semester credit hours of upper-level courses.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level or higher courses.
120 semester credit hours required for graduation
