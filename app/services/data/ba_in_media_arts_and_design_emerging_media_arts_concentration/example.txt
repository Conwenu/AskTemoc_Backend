NOTES:
1	Curriculum Requirements can be fulfilled by other approved courses from accredited institutions of higher education. The courses listed are recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy both Core Curriculum and Major Requirements at UT Dallas.
2	Core Courses are required by the state of Texas. Contact your advisor to confirm if you have any transferrable credits or test credits that may fulfill core requirements.
3	Foundations - 12 semester credit hours - Includes ATCM 2300, ATCM 2301, ATCM 2302 and choose one course from the following: ATCM 2305, ATCM 2324, ATCM 2335, or ATCM 3335
4	Fundamentals - 9 semester credit hours - Choose three courses from the following two groups:

Choose 2 courses - ATCM 2340, ATCM 2345, ATCM 2350, or ATCM 2355
Choose 1 course   - ATCM 2322 or ATCM 2343

5	Upper-Division Requirements - 16 semester credit hours - Includes ATCM 3301, ATCM 4384, and 9 additional semester credit hours selected from the following three groups:

Choose 3 courses - ATCM 3345, ANGM 3346, ATCM 3350, ATCM 3355, ATCM 3356, ATCM 3357
Choose 2 courses - ATCM 4330, ANGM 4346, ATCM 4350, ATCM 4351, ATCM 4357
Choose 1 course   - ATCM 4397, ATCM 4398, ATCM 4399

6	Prescribed Electives - 15 semester credit hours - Must all be upper-level (3000 or 4000) and 6 semester credit hours must be taken as 4000-level. Course options can be found on the second page of your digital degree plan.
7	Free Electives - 15 semester credit hours - Must be 3000- or 4000-level courses taken at UT Dallas or may be fulfilled by upper-level courses from accredited institutions of higher education.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level or higher courses.
120 semester credit hours required for graduation
