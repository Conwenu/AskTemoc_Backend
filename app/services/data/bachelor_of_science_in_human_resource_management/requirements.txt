III. Elective Requirements: 2-5 semester credit hours

Both lower- and upper-division courses may count as electives.

The plan must include sufficient upper-division courses to total 45 upper-division semester credit hours.

1. Indicates a prerequisite class to be completed before enrolling for upper-division classes.

4. BPS 4395 can be taken instead of OBHR 4395 only if OBHR 4395 is not available.

5. Students may fulfill the internship requirement with OBHR 4090, or OBHR 4V90 (1-3 semester credit hours). The zero semester credit hour courses OBHR 4090 is recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy this requirement.
