NOTES:
1	Curriculum Requirements can be fulfilled by other approved courses from accredited institutions of higher education. The courses listed are recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy both Core Curriculum and Major Requirements at UT Dallas.
2	Students completing the 020 requirement with a MATH course must complete ALEKS exam prior to enrollment. PHIL 2303 does not require the ALEKS exam.
3	Lower-Division Major Req - 13 semester credit hours beyond Core Curriculum

AHTC 1100 and ARTS 2380 and
3 semester credit hours from the following history/theory courses (AHST 1303, AHST 1304, AHST 2331, or FILM 2332) and
9 semester credit hours from the following studio courses (ARTS 1316, ARTS 2315, ARTS 2316, ARTS 2348, ARTS 2350, or ARTS 2381).
NOTE: If the student took one of the listed history/theory courses as their 050 core, then the student may choose any free elective in its place as a lower-level major requirement.

4	Major Core - 12 semester credit hours

VPAS 3340, VPAS 4389, and (VPAS 4390 or VPAS 4V99) and
3 semester credit hours of upper-level Related Courses from AHTC (excluding the ARTS prefix)

5	Concentration - 27 semester credit hours

3 semester credit hours from the following: (AHST 3318, AHST 3319, AHST 3324, or upper-level FILM course) and
3 semester credit hours of upper-level AHST or FILM course and
6 semester credit hours of 3000-level courses from Visual Arts studios and
3 semester credit hours of upper-level courses from Visual Arts studios and
6 semester credit hours of 4000-level courses from Visual Arts studios and
6 semester credit hours of upper-level Visual and Performing Arts electives

8	Free Elective - 26 semester credit hours - Any lower or upper-level course. Students may need to take some as upper-level to meet the university requirement of earning a minimum of 45 semester credit hours of upper-level courses.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level or higher courses.
120 semester credit hours required for graduation
