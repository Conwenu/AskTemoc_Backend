4	Prerequisites for Major Core and Major Related:

IMS 3310 prerequisite: BA 1310 or BA 1320 or ECON 2301
MECO 4303 prerequisites: (BA 1310 or ECON 2302) and (BA 1320 or ECON 2301)

6	MATH 1325 is required for all JSOM undergraduate students. Students may substitute MATH 2413 or MATH 2417 for MATH 1325.
7	To make timely degree progress, students should complete MATH 1325 or its equivalent by the end of their first semester at UT Dallas. Students who will not meet this requirement should contact their academic advisor to discuss their degree timeline.
8	JSOM first-time-in-college freshmen are required to take BCOM 1300 in their first semester. Transfer students and students new to JSOM are required to take BCOM 3300 in their first semester.
9	Free Elective: 18 semester credit hours are required for the B.S. in Global Business. Both lower- and upper-division courses may count as electives but students must complete at least 45 semester credit hours of upper-division courses to qualify for graduation.
10	Concentration: 12 semester credit hours are required for the Business Economics Concentration. Students must complete FIN 3350, MECO 4303, MECO 4351, and MECO 4352. Any remaining Elective hours may be used towards the required internship and community engagement experience.
11	An Internship (Practicum) of at least 160 working hours is required for all JSOM majors. IMS 4090 or BA 4090 for 0 credit hours will fulfill degree requirement only. IMS 4V90 or BA 4V90 for credit hours (1-3) can apply towards Electives. Students who are employed in full-time jobs may fulfill their internship requirement with their current employer as a special project with preapproval to register for BA 4090 or BA 4V90 from their program director.
12	IMS 4090 or BA 4090 is required if not completing IMS 4V90 or BA 4V90 for Elective credit. Students who are employed in full-time jobs may fulfill their internship requirement with their current employer as a special project with preapproval to register for BA 4090 or BA 4V90 from their program director.
13	A Community Engagement Experience is required for all JSOM majors in fall 2018 catalog or later. Students may fulfill requirement with BPS 4396 or ENTP 4340 or IMS 4335 or MKT 4360 for 3 semester credit hours Elective, or with BA 4095 upon completion of 100 service hours for 0-hour degree requirement only.
14	BA 4095 is required, if not completing BPS 4396 or ENTP 4340 or IMS 4335 or MKT 4360 for a 3 semester credit hours Elective. Students will be registered for BA 4095 for 0 SCH in the semester they complete 100 service hours.
15	This course can fulfill elective credit if not used in major related core, or elsewhere on degree plan.
16	BA 4090 or BA 4V90 will not fulfill the international experience requirement. Double major students are recommended to complete IMS 4090 or IMS 4V90 to satisfy both internship and international experience requirements.
17	Students may substitute IMS 4V94.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level and higher courses.
