NOTES:
1	Curriculum Requirements can be fulfilled by other approved courses from accredited institutions of higher education. The courses listed are recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy both Core Curriculum and Major Requirements at UT Dallas.
2	Students completing the 020 requirement with a MATH course must complete ALEKS exam prior to enrollment. PHIL 2303 does not require the ALEKS exam.
3	Lower-Division Major Req - 1 semester credit hours beyond Core Curriculum - AHTC 1100, RHET 1302, and (COMM 1311 or COMM 1315). NOTE: If the student took RHET 1302 or (COMM 1311 or COMM 1315) as their core, then the student may choose any free elective in its place as a lower-level major requirement.
4	Major Core - 12 semester credit hours - VPAS 3340, VPAS 4389, Capston/Honors (VPAS 4390 or VPAS 4V99), and 3 semester credit hours of upper-level Related Courses from AHTC (excluding the COMM prefix) Humanities that is not with the COMM prefix).
5	Concentration - 24 semester credit hours - COMM 2351, COMM 4360, COMM 4314, and 9 semester credit hours of 3000 or 4000-level course from the prefix COMM, and 6 semester credit hours of 3000 or 4000-level courses with a prefix from AHST, ARTS, COMM, DANC, FILM, MUSI, THEA, and VPAS.
6	Free Elective - 41 semester credit hours - any lower or upper-level course. Students may need to take some as upper-level to meet the university requirement of earning a minimum of 45 semester credit hours of upper-level courses.

Be sure to check prerequisites of 2000-level or higher courses.
