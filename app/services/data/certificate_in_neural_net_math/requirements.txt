Certificates

Certificate in Behavioral Research Methods

6 semester credit hours

Behavioral Research Design and Analysis, formulating and testing meaningful behavioral hypotheses, interpreting and communicating research findings, and scientific writing skills applicable to reporting data analyses. These skills are relevant to students pursuing careers in areas such as: UX Research, Data Science, and Machine Learning.

Admission Requirements

Students may register for the program in the same semester they begin taking the applicable courses, but must do so before Census Day of that term.

Prerequisites

The prerequisite for this sequence requires completion of any one of the following four courses:

PSY 2317 Statistics for Psychology

or STAT 1342 Statistical Decision Making

or CS 3341 Probability and Statistics in Computer Science and Software Engineering

or STAT 4351 Probability

Required Courses:

PSY 3392 Research Design and Analysis1

PSY 3393 Experimental Projects in Psychology1

or CGS 3340 Experimental Projects in Cognitive Science1

Certificate in Biobehavioral Data Science

6 semester credit hours

Introduces important tools and methods in the areas of Data Science and Machine Learning. These skills are relevant to students pursuing careers in Data Science and Machine Learning.

Admission Requirements

Students may register for the program in the same semester they begin taking the applicable courses, but must do so before Census Day of that term.

Prerequisites

No computer programming experience is required.

Required Courses:

CGS 3346 PYTHON for Biobehavioral Data Analysis1

CGS 3342 Cognitive and Neural Modeling Laboratory1

Certificate in User Experience (UX) Research

6 semester credit hours

Introduces important tools and methods relevant in the pursuit of careers in User Experience (UX) Research and Usability Engineering.

Admission Requirements

Students may register for the program in the same semester they begin taking the applicable courses, but must do so before Census Day of that term.

Prerequisites

No computer programming experience is assumed.

Required Courses:

CGS 4353 Qualitative UX Research1

CGS 4321 Quantitative UX Research1

Certificate in Neural Net Math

6 semester credit hours

The following two course sequence (which is cross-listed with the UTD Computer Science department and the UTD Cognitive Science Program) provides an in-depth introduction to mathematics relevant to understanding advanced machine learning algorithms and data science concepts.

Admission Requirements

Students may register for the program in the same semester they begin taking the applicable courses, but must do so before Census Day of that term.

Prerequisites

The following three courses are prerequisites for this sequence:

MATH 2414 Integral Calculus

or MATH 2419 Calculus II

or MATH 3351 Advanced Calculus

MATH 2418 Linear Algebra

CS 3341 Probability and Statistics in Computer Science and Software Engineering

or STAT 4351 Probability

Required Courses:

CS 4314 Intelligent Systems Analysis1

or CGS 4314 Intelligent Systems Analysis1

CS 4315 Intelligent Systems Design1

or CGS 4315 Intelligent Systems Design1

1. Required courses must be completed with at least a grade of B.
