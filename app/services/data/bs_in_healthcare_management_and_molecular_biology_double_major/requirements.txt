1. Degree is 153 -154 semester credit hours if students are required to take NATS 1101.

2. Indicates a prerequisite class to be completed before enrolling for upper-division classes.

3. A required Major course that also fulfills a Core Curriculum requirement. Semester credit hours are counted in Core Curriculum.

4. Six semester credit hours of Calculus are counted under Mathematics Core and Component Area Option Core, and 2 semester credit hours of Calculus are counted as Biology Major Preparatory Courses.

5. MATH 2413 is required for the Healthcare Management/Molecular Biology prescribed double major. Students may elect to substitute MATH 2417 for MATH 2413.

6. To make timely degree progress, students should complete MATH 2413 or MATH 2417 by the end of their first semester at UT Dallas. Students who will not meet this requirement should contact their academic advisor to discuss their degree timeline.

7. Certain courses listed are prerequisites for major core, major concentration, or major related courses. Choose accordingly.

8. Students may elect to substitute MATH 2419 for MATH 2414.

9. Students who complete PHYS 2421 do not need to complete PHYS 2125.

10. BPS 4395 can be taken instead of HMGT 4395 only if HMGT 4395 is not available.

11. Students may fulfill the internship requirement with HMGT 4090, or HMGT 4V90 (1-3 semester credit hours). The zero semester credit hour course HMGT 4090 is recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy this requirement.

12. Students may fulfill the community engagement requirement with BPS 4396 or BA 4095, IMS 4335, ENTP 4340, MKT 4360, or OPRE 4391. The zero semester credit hour course BA 4095 is recommended as the most efficient way to satisfy this requirement.

13. Requires permission of the Biology Undergraduate Advisor to ensure training in recombinant DNA analysis.
